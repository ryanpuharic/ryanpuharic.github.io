Public Access



about

✦ Public Access is a rock band that makes westerns. We formed in 2017 in the hallway between math class and the gym. Since then my friends and I have released 122 minutes of music and played shows across the northeast. I play bass in the band but also sing, produce, engineer, and write a lot of the songs.
